Nasa Moon Website Card Sorting

Week 5 Project

This week we card sorted Nasa's Moon Website, when I did the card sorting for the first time, I was confused for a number of reasons:

  • Why wasn't Neil Armstrong under the "People" section?
  • What did "Concepts" mean? under the "Missions and Programs" section.
  • Why was "Products" under the "Multi-Media" section?
  • Shouldn't "Get Involved" be under "Multi-Media?"

Once I did the revisions, I removed Neil Armstrong and placed his content inside of the "People" section. I also moved "Get Involved" under the "Multi-Media" section, because multi-media could include social media..etc. Finally, I removed the "Concepts" and "Products" sections, because I didn't think they were relevant.
